ABOUT ME

I have always loved tinkering with electronics and experimenting with software. As a kid and up to my teenage years, I played computer games in my free time. I researched and studied the vast variety of computer parts and learnt the best specifications a computer should have in order to run demanding programmes at optimal settings. I moved on to helping my peers build their PCs. I found out that different users look for different things in their PCs; some want high-end GPUs and CPUs, while others just want something within their budgets. I learnt to look for the optimal specifications for PCs for users with different demands.

I believe in incorporating our interests and passions into our work life, hence I am choosing Computer Engineering as my choice of study, since it involves me incorporating my passion, computers, into my career.
